Getting Started

1️⃣

Add Your Habits

Click "+ Add Habit" or "Today's Habit" in the sidebar to create your first habit. Give it a name and emoji!

2️⃣

Track Daily

Check off habits each day from the Dashboard or Calendar view. Watch your progress bar fill up!

3️⃣

View Reports

Check the Reports section to see weekly & monthly trends. Identify your strongest and weakest habits.

4️⃣

Take Notes

Use the Notes section to log daily reflections, motivations, or reminders to stay on track.
